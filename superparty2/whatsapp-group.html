Frequently Asked Questions


What is the maximum number of guest per party?

We accept orders for any number of guests, but for a guest size of more than 200 individualas, we recommend scheduling the event well in advance so that we can pull off a smooth party.

Do you provide global delivery?

Currently, we are delivering across US and Canada, we will soon be delivering across the globe. You can check the deliverable locations in product delivery system.

What are the mode of payment?

We accept both debit and credit card payment.

What is the minimum time required to schedule an online party?

For a group of 200 guests, we need a minimum of 3 days time to schedule an online event, and for more than 200 people we need a minimum of 5 days.

Can I reschedule the party?

Yes you can but only if you choose to do so in 24 hours advance.

Can you deliver orders in different time zone as per different groups?

Yes, all of the delivery will be made in your selected time as per your group choice.

What if an order is not delivered?

This does not happen, but if we meet any contingent situation we will keep you informed well in advance and an alternate arrangement will be made mutually.
