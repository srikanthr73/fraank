Frequently Asked Questions

Host FAQ
How immediately you can schedule a virtual event?

We need a minimum of 24 hours to schedule an event but it will be great if you could give us at least 48 hours to schedule a virtual order. We believe in making an event memorable for you and for your guests. All guests will be notified about the scheduled delivery in advance.

What is the minimum and maximum limit of guests per event?

We can help you create an event for a minimum of 5 and there is no cap on the maximum guest’s in an event. feel free to contact to our customer support team if you have you want to organize an event for more than 1000 guests.

Can you deliver pizza, drinks, and lunch for a different groups in different slots?

Yes, you can but it depends on the number of guests per slot in a group, the choice of items available, and comes with additional operational cost. Please connect to the customer support team to get the quotation.

Can I reschedule my virtual event date?

Yes, you can, but only if the scheduled event’s delivery schedule is 24 hours away and the desired schedule is at least 24 hours away from the current schedule. Please connect to the customer support team to reschedule your virtual event.

What is the ideal time to schedule a virtual event?

For a group of fewer than 100 individuals, we accept an order in 24 hours advance, but if you want to schedule a party for a group of more than 100 individuals we request you give us at least 48 hours to make sure that every thing is well planned and orders are delivered on time.

Can I add new add-ons, drinks, and experience to my existing package?

Yes, you can, for food and drink only if your event is still 24 hours away from the additional request time. And, for experience, your request will only be entertained 48 hours and advance.

Do I need to pay any additional cost for a party of large group?

What is the cancelation policy?

You can cancel your order 24 hours before the scheduled delivery time for the order and get a 100% refund on your deposit. In case you choose to cancel in less than 24 hours, no refund will be given.

What if my order does not get delivered?

If you are present at the order delivery location and do not receive your order for more than 30 minutes after the scheduled delivery time, please connect with the customer support team and register a complaint. Our team will act on your complaint and will do the needful. For any complaint registered after 24 hours of the scheduled delivery time, we will not be able to help you or compensate in any form.

Do I get any refund in case one or more items are missing from any order?

Yes, in case of any missing order please write to our customer support team and we will compensate for the same. You need to connect with our customer support team within the first 3 hours of order delivery, we will not be able to process any compensation if you register a complain post 3 hours of scheduled delivery.

I received an order with wrong name.

Do not worry, just connect with our customer support team and we will guide you through. It can also be a clerical mistake, we just need to assure that it was from the right planner and the package is yours.

What if I received the wrong order?

If the order is not the same as your planner menu than immediately connect with our customer support team and we will arrange the replacement at the earliest. Please raise your complaint within 3 hours of the delivery, in case of nay delay we will not be able to provide any replacement or refund.

What are the deliverables locations?

Delivery of the order depends on the items selected, for example

Pizza & Coffee and breakfast

Currently, we deliver pizza only in US and Canada. We will be taking global orders soon.

Drink

Currently, we deliver drinks in all locations where alcohol delivery is allowed in US and Canada. We will be taking global orders soon.

Lunch

Currently, we deliver lunch only in US and Canada. We will be taking global orders soon.

What if the super party can not deliver to a guest?

We have a widespread presence in US and Canada and are delivering in almost every location but in case any of your guests are staying in non-deliverable locations, we will inform you and the guest in advance. If you wish, we can send a virtual gift card to your guest redeemable at any pizza center.

What if the order was never delivered?

This does not happen so easily, but if it does happen we can understand how frustrating it can get. Under such circumstances, we request you and your guest to inform us within 3 hours of the scheduled event time and we will make the required check. And, will process the refund if need be.

Who will be hosting my virtual experience?

We will be sending you all the needed information well in advance, we have a team who takes care of planning, scheduling, and hosting fun-filled experiences for all of our virtual events.

Can I connect with the host before the scheduled event?

Yes, we will introduce experience host to you and you can have answers to all of your query.

What if we do not like the experience?

We share the proposed experience list with you, well in advance, and upon your confirmation only we deliver the experience, but we understand at times things do not turn up the way we plan. Please feel free to share the feedback and our team will get back to you with the scope of improvements.

Do you deliver in the rural area?

In case you are able to deliver a pizza to your present address, then yes we will be able to. We are most likely able to deliver an order to your doorstep. Click here to check the available delivery locations

Can I change my order?

Yes, you can but only if you place a request before 24 hours of the scheduled event time, and for this please connect with our customer support team.

What personal details you need to deliver the order?

We need the guest’s name, delivery address, and contact details to confirm the availability at the desired time. Stay assured your personal details will not be shared with any third party.
