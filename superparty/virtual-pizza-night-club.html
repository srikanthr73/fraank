Our Favourite Virtual Party Ideas, Games, and Activities

It’s virtual gaming time! Here our cool gaming stuff for you “without gaming stuff party will always get bored” so we always keep you excited with our stuff with online gatherings

Here our cool gaming stuff

Trivia Night

Trivia works great either as the focus of your online event or as a cosy icebreaker. This activity naturally fits for your online occasion

Simply you have to gather a list about pizza –related facts, and create a quiz game. Players can compete by completing a form in breakout room, using Kahoot! To answers via mobile device or being the first to the type correct answers into the chat

Watch-Along Movie with your cheese slice

There’s nothing quite like watching a movie with friends. “Sharing Hot Pizza Slice with your team and watching Movie together makes you crazy right” this is what always makes you crazy. In this activity the host or some other person will share the screen. Here we ask some questions about the film after the movie gets completed or 30 min pause the discussion get started about the movie via chat.

Pizza Eating Contest

Pizza eating contests puts you in a competitive spirit among your team. To make this contest fair everyone make sure to use same pizza size. To start this contest, set a time limit and then make your contestants get ready to eat. Who eats more pizza or who completes their pizza within a time they win super cool stuff.

Karaoke

Do you really wish you could sing right now, shouting into microphones as the world around you? If your answer is yes Why can’t you always hit the karaoke bar together; This is a lively event and is ideal if your team is full of confident people who love to sing. You’ll need someone to act as the karaoke DJ, a playlist of hits to sing along to, and plenty of enthusiasm.

Scavenger Hunt

Get your virtual party guests up and moving with a hunt around their home things

Scavenger hunt list where the host reads off items one by one and the first guest to retrieve the object and show it on the screen earns one point for themselves or their team. You could also share the list of items on screen or in the chat and give the team 5mints time to retrieve the object as possible.

Virtual Escape Room

If your guests love all things mystery, suspense, and problem solving, a virtual escape room could be the ideal event. Your team can pull together to help find their way out before the timer ends.

Book with an escape room provider, and they’ll have everything set up for your guests all you need to do is virtually show up. This works great as a team building activity
